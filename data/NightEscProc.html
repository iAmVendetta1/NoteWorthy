Night Escalation Procedure

Escalate to Help Desk Mid-Shift

For tickets that require work to be done tonight:
- Must be escalated before 4:00 PM (CDT)
- Contact Help Desk Supervisor for exception

PROCESS:
Step 1: Copy and paste the below template into a new case event. Save as a billable note.
Step 2: E-mail the client advising that the case is going to be worked by our night Team.
Step 3: Escalate ticket to Help Desk Supervisors for case re-assignment.

Send to Help Desk Night Queue

For tickets that are not time critical.

PROCESS:

Case 1: Task or procedure can be completed without client intervention:
1. Write a brief summary including the issue and what you have done so far.
2. Add a description of what you would like done including pertitent information.
3. Escalate the ticket to your Help Desk Supervisor for re-assignment.

Case 2: You are unsure of what to do, need direction, or need research done:
1. Write a brief summary including the issue and what you have done so far.
2. Ask for advice/direction or if research can be done on the topic.
3. Escalate the ticket to your Help Desk Supervisor for re-assignment.
